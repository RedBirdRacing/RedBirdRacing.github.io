About Us

The Official Formula Student Racing Team of The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.

Who are we?

Originated as an experimental project in 2017, Red Bird Racing (EVRT) became the official student racing team of HKUST. The team actively participates in Formula Students, one of the most established engineering competitions in the world. Through this competition, the team aims to develop the knowledge and skills of aspiring engineers to succeed in their careers.


What is our goal?

Our main goal is to compete in Formula Student United Kingdom 2022 (FSUK 2022), Class 1.

Throughout the development, we aim to provide a platform for students to learn by doing and step out of their comfort zone, so as to explore what they are truly capable of and mature further.


What kind of members do we have?

Our team is composed of students from the School of Engineering, Science and Business & Management. We welcome everyone from every field as we believe every one can contribute as long as they are passionate.





What is inside Formula Student?

Formula Student is Europe's most established educational engineering competition which uses motorsport to inspire students.

It requires teams to design, build, test, and race a Formula-style race car while making sure that there is a sound business model to back up a hypothetical mass-production line.


The main event includes:

  • Design Class
  • Dynamic events (0-75m, Skid-pad, Endurance)
  • Business Presentation
